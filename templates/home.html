SchedTalk was created to make your life easier. We know how frustrating scheduling can be, so we built SchedTalk: An Intelligent Scheduling Chatbot for Booking, Rescheduling, and Managing Consultation Appointments to help you book, reschedule, and manage appointments quickly and effortlessly, just by chatting.
